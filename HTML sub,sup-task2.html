CYBER SECURITY

ABOUT

Cyber security is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It's also known as technology security or electronic information security.

IMPORTANCE

Cyber security refers toprotecting systems connected to the internet from threats in cyberspace, some of them business-critical. It involves protecting an organization's software, data, and hardware and helps prevent cybercriminals from gaining access to devices or their networks.
